Columbia Raas
CU Raas is Columbia University's premier South Asian dance team.
Founded in August
2003
Columbia Raas was established in the fall of 2003 to celebrate the tradition and energetic style of Garba-Raas. CU Raas has performed at various venues both on and off campus, and has competed in Garba Raas competitions across the nation for many years. Past competition venues include California, North California, and Texas. CU Raas strives to be an exciting addition to the diversity of dance styles and cultural experiences available for their fellow New Yorkers.
